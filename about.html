About the Course

This Certificate Course in Yoga & Naturopathy is designed to introduce students to holistic healing and natural methods of health improvement. Through a combination of theoretical and practical modules, the course empowers individuals to understand and apply naturopathic principles for personal and professional well-being.

Students will learn about the five elements of nature, yogic practices, detoxification techniques, diet therapy, and other complementary therapies. Emphasis is placed on achieving harmony between body, mind, and spirit.

Course Objective

  • To provide basic knowledge of Yoga & Naturopathy
  • To promote drugless therapies for prevention and cure
  • To help students build a healthy lifestyle using natural methods

Who Should Enroll?

  • Health professionals seeking alternative methods
  • Wellness coaches and yoga teachers
  • Students passionate about natural healing
  • Anyone interested in preventive healthcare

Course Duration

6 Months (Weekend Batches)
Each session will include theoretical learning, demonstrations, and practice.

Certification

Upon successful completion of all modules and final assessment, candidates will receive a recognized certificate in Yoga & Naturopathy.
